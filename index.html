About

Filmmaker with a technical perspective.

Portrait coming soon

Samuel Caruthers is a Los Angeles-based writer and director with degrees in Film & Media Studies and Statistics & Data Science from UC Santa Barbara.

He has written and directed three short films and has supported feature development, casting, production, and executive operations at INTERDEPENDENT Studio.

His creative work is informed by an interest in technology, changing workplaces, ambition, relationships, and the ways people search for meaning in rapidly evolving environments.
